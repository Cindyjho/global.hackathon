Help us improve our results

If you notice something off in our results – maybe we graded one concept too harshly for this TOS, or maybe we graded it too leniently – you can help improve our model by letting us know.

Once you turn on Feedback Mode by clicking the button below, hovering over a concept will reveal some tools:

[screenshot]

Clicking the up arrow will tell us that this TOS is actually better on this concept than we think.

Clicking the down arrow will tell us that this TOS is worse on this concept than we think.

Once you're done evaluating concepts, click "Submit Feedback" in the footer where you originally launched this modal:

[screenshot]
